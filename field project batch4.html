    How Our Delivery Agents Work

    Our delivery agents are trained to ensure your food reaches you fresh and on time. Here's how it works:

    • Step 1: Place your order on our website or app.
    • Step 2: Our kitchen prepares your food with care.
    • Step 3: A delivery agent picks up your order and ensures it's packed securely.
    • Step 4: The agent delivers your order to your doorstep within the estimated time.
    • Step 5: You receive a notification when the order is delivered.
    • Step 6: Enjoy your meal and leave feedback!

    We guarantee a hassle-free delivery experience with real-time tracking and updates.
